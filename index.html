How it works

Three steps,
and you're protected.

1

Order the tracker

A tiny, waterproof device. Clip it to a collar, sew it into clothing, or attach it to a backpack.

2

Draw your zone

In the app, draw any shape around the playground, yard, or school. Not a circle, not a rectangle — exactly what you want.

3

Live your life

If they leave the zone, we notify you instantly. If you want to call them home, one button triggers an alert on the tracker.
